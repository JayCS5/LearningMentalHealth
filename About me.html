I'm a 16 year old high school student in my senior year. I'm located in Belize City, Belize, and I decided to create this website in order to promote and educate about mental health especially for those in developing countries. Mental health involves one's social, psychological and emotional well-being. Society often portrays sharing our emotions and feelings as weak; however, I am of the opinion that it is the opposite. I think that having the bravery to express what you feel is one of the best and bravest things you can do for yourself. It's ok to not be okay and it's ok to need help. We all deserve happiness. Thoughts like these influenced my mission statement which is "Achieving happiness one breath at a time".
